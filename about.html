About Us

A Taste of Tradition, Straight from Ambur At Ambur Biryani, we bring you the legendary flavors of one of South India’s most cherished culinary treasures — the world-famous Ambur Biryani. Originating from the small town of Ambur in Tamil Nadu, this aromatic delicacy has a history that dates back over a century, blending Mughlai influences with traditional South Indian spices.

The Heart of Our Cooking

We use the signature Seeraga Samba rice, known for its delicate aroma and ability to soak up rich, flavorful gravies. Our spice blends are freshly ground every day, using traditional methods to ensure unmatched aroma and taste. Each pot of biryani is slow-cooked to perfection, allowing the flavors of tender meat, earthy spices, and fragrant rice to merge beautifully. From the first whiff to the last bite, our goal is to give you an authentic taste that feels like home — whether you’re dining in with family or taking a meal to share with friends.

Our Story

We started as a family recipe, passed down through generations, has now become a symbol of authentic taste and soulful cooking. Every plate we serve carries the legacy of time-honored preparation — from slow-cooked, tender meat to fragrant seeraga samba rice, infused with freshly ground spices and love.

Our Promise

We believe great food is more than just taste — it’s about memories, culture, and togetherness. That’s why we use only the freshest ingredients, handpicked spices, and traditional cooking methods to ensure that every bite transports you straight to the bustling streets of Ambur.
